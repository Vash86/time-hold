Overview

Time Hold is a simple offline timing game. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the app to work entirely without collecting any personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data, including but not limited to:

The app stores your game scores locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Time Hold does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. The app works entirely offline with no internet connection required.

This app contains no advertisements.

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
